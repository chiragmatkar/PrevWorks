COVID Progression Back To Work

  1. Day 1 Test positive for COVID. Submitted COVID injury report and rec
  2. Days 2-9 Quarantine and isolate.
  3. Day 10 Take a PCR COVID-19 test.
  4. Day 11 Given a negative test and if there are no symptoms, you are free to end isolation and return to work.
  5. Days 11-20 Those who tested positive and have moderate to mild symptoms can end isolation after 10 days if at least 24 hours have passed without a fever and other symptoms have improved.
  6. Day 14 Those who were exposed to the virus but were never tested and have no symptoms should continue to quarantine for the full 14 days.
  7. Day 20 Those who tested positive and have severe illness may need to continue isolation for a full 20 days.
